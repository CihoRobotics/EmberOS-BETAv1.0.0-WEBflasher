FAQ — Common questions
Which browsers work?
Google Chrome and Microsoft Edge version 89 or newer. Firefox and Safari cannot access USB serial ports. A desktop or laptop is required — mobile will not work.
My board is not showing in the port list.
Hold the BOOT button while clicking Install. If it still does not show, your cable may be charge-only — try a different cable. You may also need a USB driver: search CP210x or CH340 plus your OS name.
Where is the full documentation?
The complete user manual with all commands, sensor guides and wiring diagrams is at github.com/CihoRobotics/EmberOS.
Is Ember OS free?
Yes. Fully open source under GPL-3.0. Everything is free at github.com/CihoRobotics/EmberOS.
